About Us

Elgris Solar Power Systems


ELGRIS Solar was established in the year of 2014 by Mr. Sampatkumar Dhoot, a Mechanical Engineer with Masters in Renewable Energy System at Aachen University, Germany. ELGRIS is global pioneering leader in the On Grid Solar Roof Top System. Elgris provides end-to-end solutions that cover the entire value chain of engineering offering to serve the needs of Solar to its various customers.
